About

I'm more than just a dedicated engineer. I enjoy rock climbing, mountaineering, & hiking. In fact, my friends and I made an impromptu trip to Ecuador to climb Cotopaxi, a 19,347 ft active volcano. I have always valued nature and want to see it protected.
When I'm not scaling mountians, you'll find me in the water, jet skiing, sailing, surfing, and scuba diving. I love hands-on work and tackling new challenges, whether it's using a CNC Mill or a pottery wheel.

My goal is to combine my engineering expertise with my passions, and I can't wait to get started.

Datsun 240z Restoration

I have been completely restorationing a vintage 1973 Datsun 240Z, and it's been an incredible journey. From engine overhauls to deciphering complicated wiring diagrams, I've tackled nearly every aspect of the rebuild. This hands-on experience has taught me a lot about problem-solving, patience, and the importance of precision and accuracy. It's been a labor of love. This project has strengthened my technical abilities and has given me a deep appreciation for how every component of a system needs to work together.
