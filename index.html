About

I'm more than just a dedicated engineer— I enjoy rock climbing, mountaineering, & hiking. In fact, my friends and I made an impromptu trip to Ecuador to climb Cotopaxi, a 19,347 ft active volcano. I have always valued nature and want to see it protected.

My goal is to combine my engineering expertise with my passions, and I can't wait to get started.

Experience

Solar Turbines (Caterpillar)

Supply Chain Technical Engineer

June 2024 - Present

I worked with suppliers of remanufactured turbine parts to enhance yield through process improvements, optimizing part recovery rates and minimizing waste. I collaborated with both internal engineering teams and external suppliers to develop and implement new remanufacturing processes, improving efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and overall quality in turbine component restoration. Additionally, I performed a full disassembly and assembly of a Titan turbine engine, gaining in-depth, hands-on experience with critical hardware. This allowed me to develop a comprehensive understanding of component interactions, failure modes, and best practices for maintaining and improving turbine performance in demanding operational environments.

RedWire Space

Design Engineer

June 2023 - May 2024

I worked on the ISS solar panels and Lunar Gateway's Power and Propulsion Element roll-out panels, contributing to ground support equipment, testing equipment, and flight hardware. I had full ownership of the design and manufacturing process from concept to completion, ensuring seamless execution, quality, and efficiency at every stage. Working closely with technicians, I gained hands-on experience using laser trackers to precisely level testing hardware for zero-gravity simulations. Working at a smaller company required me to wear many hats, allowing me to gain a broad range of experience across multiple disciplines—including PCB board design!

I have been completely restoring a vintage 1973 Datsun 240Z, and it's been an incredible journey. From engine overhauls to deciphering complicated wiring diagrams, I've tackled nearly every aspect of the rebuild. This hands-on experience has taught me a lot about problem-solving, patience, and the importance of precision and accuracy. It's been a labor of love. This project has strengthened my technical abilities and has given me a deep appreciation for how every component of a system needs to work together.
